# VOICE

How Studio42 sounds. Applies to every word on the site, every email, every caption, every error message.

---

## Principles

1. **Dark and minimal.** Same aesthetic as the visual design. No warm welcome mats. No exclamation points.
2. **Material-focused.** Name the material. Describe the weight, the finish, the process. Materials are the story.
3. **Declarative.** Full stops, not commas. Short sentences.
4. **Warm but not soft.** We care. We just don't perform caring.
5. **Honest about process.** Handmade means imperfect. Say so.
6. **Specific over generic.** "Brushed aluminum from a single sheet" beats "premium materials."

## Yes / No

### Yes
- Made by hand. Imperfect on purpose.
- Material matters. Nothing else does.
- Matte black steel. Natural wood. Brushed aluminum.
- Limited run. When it's gone, it's gone.
- LIGHTING-V1. Fifty units. Ljubljana.
- Built in one studio. By one pair of hands.
- The finish is deliberate. The weight is too.
- Hand-finished. No two are identical.

### No
- We are passionate about creating beautiful handcrafted pieces that inspire joy.
- Experience the magic of artisan lighting.
- A journey into materiality.
- Elevate your space with premium craftsmanship.
- Our curated collection of timeless designs.
- Discover the story behind each piece.
- Welcome to Studio42 — where artistry meets innovation.
- Carefully curated. Thoughtfully designed. Beautifully made.

### Words on the banned list
`journey`, `passionate`, `passion`, `inspire`, `elevate`, `curated`, `thoughtfully`, `beautifully`, `timeless`, `crafted` (verb), `premium`, `luxurious`, `experience` (as a verb), `discover`, `seamless`, `artistry` (as a noun by itself)

If a draft has one of these, rewrite it.

## Surface-by-surface

### Product copy
Lead with the material. Then the process. Then the story.

> **LIGHTING-V1**
> Matte black steel. Natural wood. Brushed aluminum.
> Hand-built in Ljubljana. Fifty units.
> €50.

Avoid taglines. Avoid adjectives stacked on adjectives.

### About
Explain what Studio42 is in two sentences. Show the team. Show the process. Stop.

> Studio42 is a lighting studio in Ljubljana.
> Luka designs and builds. Zorkow draws. Zarja holds the thread.
> Every object is made by hand, in small numbers, from materials we can explain.

### Blog
First-person, Luka's voice. Short paragraphs. Photos of the work, captions that name the material.

> I started LIGHTING-V1 with a sheet of steel and a drawing.
> The drawing changed three times. The steel didn't.

### Community / contact
Direct. No "we'd love to hear from you." Just tell them how.

> Questions? Use the chat.
> Email: hello@studio42.si (when DNS is done)
> We reply within 24 hours on weekdays.

### CTAs (buttons, links)
Short. Imperative. Uppercase matches our visual type rule.

- **Yes:** `SHOP LIGHTING-V1`, `SEE THE PROCESS`, `READ ON`, `GET IN TOUCH`
- **No:** `Click here to learn more`, `Discover our collection`, `Find out more`

### Errors / empty states
Dry. Informative. No apologies.

- 404: "This page doesn't exist." (not "Oops! Lost in space?")
- Empty cart: "Nothing here yet."
- Form error: "Email is required." (not "Oh no! Please enter your email address.")

### Metadata (SEO)
Title: `Product — Studio42` format. Description: one sentence, under 155 chars, includes materials.

> **Title:** LIGHTING-V1 — Studio42
> **Description:** Handmade lamp. Matte black steel, natural wood, brushed aluminum. Limited run from Ljubljana. €50.

### Email
Same voice. No signature beyond `— Studio42` or `— Luka`. No quotes. No motivational closings.

## Tone dials

| Situation | Tone |
|---|---|
| Selling a product | Confident, specific, material-first |
| Explaining the process | Curious, workmanlike |
| Something broke | Honest, brief, no spiraling apologies |
| Welcoming | Restrained. We don't gush. |
| Saying no | Direct. "That's not something we do." |

## Slovenian context

Studio42 is Slovenian. It's okay to be a Slovenian brand speaking English — don't try to sound American.

- "Made in Ljubljana." — specifically.
- Units: metric (cm, kg, €).
- Dates: ISO (2026-05-15) or long form ("May 15, 2026"). Avoid US-style "5/15/26".
- Don't overexplain geography. Ljubljana is enough.

## Rewriting drill

Take a generic sentence. Kill the fluff. Name the material. Cut the word count in half.

**Before:**
> Each of our lamps is lovingly handcrafted by our skilled artisans using premium materials, resulting in a truly one-of-a-kind piece that will elevate any space.

**After:**
> Hand-built from matte black steel and natural wood. Fifty units. No two identical.

Apply this drill to every draft before shipping.
